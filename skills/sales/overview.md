# Feature: Sales & CRM

## Overview
Manages the entire revenue lifecycle, from lead generation and customer relationship management to order fulfillment and invoicing.

## 1. CRM (Customer Relationship Management)
- **Lead & Opportunity Tracking**: Pipeline management with Kanban boards.
- **Customer 360° View**: Unified history of interactions, orders, and documents.
- **Sales Forecasting**: Analytics based on pipeline probability.

## 2. Sales Operations (Hybrid Product Catalog)
- **Product Catalog**: Products are explicitly stored and categorized as either **Software** or **Hardware**:
  - **Software**: System modules and SaaS subscriptions (e.g., HR System, Sales System) where variants dictate the subscription term (Monthly, Yearly).
  - **Hardware**: Physical goods, accessories, phones, laptops, etc., where variants dictate physical specifications (Color, Storage) and require inventory tracking.
- **Quotation Management**: Professional quotes capable of mixing Hardware and Software on the same document.
- **Sales Order Processing**: Automated conversion from quotation to order, triggering both fulfillment (for Hardware) and provisioning (for Software).
- **Revenue Recognition**: Integration with FMS for automated accounting entries.

## 3. Subscription & Billing
- **Recurring Billing**: Automate monthly/annual subscription invoices.
- **Contract Management**: Track renewals and expiration alerts.
